About Us

Tanza Doctors Hospital, Inc. (TDHI)

is a Level II private hospital established by a group of dedicated businessman and medical professionals committed to delivering modern and high-quality healthcare services. Strategically located in the heart of Tanza, Cavite - a rapidly growing residential and industrial community - the hospital sits on a 4,000-square-meter property along A. Soriano Highway, Barangay Sahud-Ulan. TDHI operates within a six-story faciliy with a total floor area of 9,600 square meters and a capacity of 105 beds, equipped with up-to-date medical technologies to serve the healthcare needs of the community.

Departments

Our hospital offers a wide range of specialized medical services, each dedicated to providing expert care and improving patient well-being.

Cardiology

Focused on heart health, our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for cardiovascular conditions, from prevention to advanced treatment.

Gastroenterology

Specializing in digestive health, this department manages conditions affecting the stomach, intestines, liver, and related organs.

Pulmonology

Dedicated to respiratory care, our pulmonology team treats lung and breathing disorders with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic approaches.

Dental

Providing oral health services, the dental department offers preventive care, restorative treatments, and cosmetic procedures.

Gynecology

Focused on women’s health, our gynecology department provides care across all stages of life, including reproductive and preventive services.

Hepatology

Specializing in liver health, the hepatology department manages conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and other liver-related diseases.

Prepare for visit

Visiting the hospital can feel overwhelming, but being prepared makes the experience smoother. Patients are encouraged to bring documents, a list of current medications, and any questions they may have for their doctor. Our team is here to guide you every step of the way, ensuring your visit is comfortable and stress-free.

Surgery proccess

Surgery is a significant step in a patient’s care journey. This article outlines the typical stages: pre-operative consultation, the procedure itself, and recovery. By providing clear information and support, we aim to help patients and families feel confident and reassured throughout the process.
